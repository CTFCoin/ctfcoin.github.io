CTFCoin

The world's only blockchain solution for security competitions.

Seamlessly reward a cryptographically secure, blockchain based currency as an incentive for your CTFs. Allow donations to CTFs with CTFCoin to help users incentivize higher quality competitions.

The ultimate solution to CTF funding

Problem?

There currently exists no way besides cash and non-purpose built crypto-currencies to reward competitive security players during competitions. In addition, there is no way to incentivize higher quality CTFs besides ratings on CTF-Time.

Consequence?

Our in-house research shows that the average CTF spends a large portion of its CTF-Time figuring out how to send out cash rewards and verifying information of recepients.

Solution

That is why we created CTFCoin, a simple, highly-intuitive, cryptographically secure, blockchain-verified, expertly designed and coded currency explicitly for CTFs.
